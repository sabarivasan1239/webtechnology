ABOUT US

Domino's is an Ann Arbor-based American multinational pizza restaurant chain. Founded in 1960, the chain is owned by master franchisor Domino's Pizza, Inc.[3] and led by CEO Russell Weiner. The corporation is Delaware-domiciled[4] and headquartered at the Domino's Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor Township, near Ann Arbor, Michigan.[5][6] As of 2018, Domino's had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the United States, 1,500 in India, and 1,249 in the United Kingdom.[7][8] Domino's has stores in over 83 countries[9] and 5,701 cities worldwide.
